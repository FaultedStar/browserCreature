# Creature Template

A p5.js starter template for building a digital creature that lives on your portfolio website.

## Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in your browser to see the creature
2. Click on the creature to feed it
3. Edit `sketch.js` to customise your creature

## Basic creature 
It wants to be clicked on to stay satified. 
It watches the mouse pointer. 

It knows when it's browser tab is not the focus - it's satisfaction depleates faster. 

It knows how many times you have visited it, and keeps this infomation saved. 

## ☀️ Fancy creature ☀️
Knows the location specific weather, and the time of day. Has ambiant animations based on these. Weather can be spoofed in z_weather.js file. 

It can send system notification when it finds magic coloured stones. 

## Expantion possiblitys 

Could be connected to any API! To push updates to SM, and to respond to other external stimuli.

It could be further gamified.

It could be built to be more of a simulator, with multiple creatures exhibiting flocking behavior or social structures. 

It could have more nuanced interaction elements, such as responding to a webcam or other sensors. 

It could produce artifacts of its own based on treatments or inputs.
